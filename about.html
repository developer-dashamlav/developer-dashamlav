Story

  • Dashamlav traces its origins to the vision of our leading partner, Preshit Jaiswal. With a rich background in companies like Selzer and Modibond, Preshit made a significant impact in the realm of online goods sales. Drawing from his experiences, he envisioned a brand that would redefine standards – a brand that would become Dashamlav.

  • At the core of Dashamlav's inception are three fundamental principles: reduced overheads, increased profits, and data-driven intelligent decisions. These principles are symbolized by our distinctive logo featuring three circles coinciding at the center, representing the harmony of efficiency, profitability, and analytics.

  • Preshit's journey from shaping success in established companies to founding Dashamlav reflects a commitment to innovation and a passion for making a real difference in the online marketplace. Join us as we continue to pioneer new possibilities and set higher standards in the world of e-commerce.
